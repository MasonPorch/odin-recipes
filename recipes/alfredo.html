Cooked dish of alfredo

Alfredo is a classic Italian-American dish featuring a rich, creamy sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. The sauce is gently heated until it thickens, creating a velvety texture that coats tender pasta—often fettuccine—perfectly. Subtle hints of garlic, black pepper, and sometimes a pinch of nutmeg enhance its luxurious flavor. This indulgent dish is typically garnished with a sprinkle of extra Parmesan and fresh parsley, offering a comforting and satisfying meal beloved for its simplicity and decadence.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic salt
  • Romano and parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
